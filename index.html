About me

Hi! My name is Karol. I am a QA Software Engineer who develops his skills as an Automation Tester and Front-End Developer. I have a 8-year professional e-commerce experience that helps me in my daily work.

Today I work as a QA Software Engineer in the biggest company creating software and electronics for analysing and calculating driver working time, dedicated to the transport industry.

I graduated from The Higher School of Economics and Computer Science in Cracow and it was my start in the IT world. IT is related to my education, work and hobbies. I try to take advantage of every spare moment to improve my skills, because I love to learn new technologies.

Oh! I have almost forgotten. I am 32 years old and I come from Czestochowa. Today, I live and work in Bielsko Biala.

In my free time I love to play the guitar. I am interested in cinema and music. I am a big fan of music recorded on vinyl. I have a small collection of them ;). If time permits, I am also passionate about the Historical Reenacting.

Thank you for visiting my website. It is an honor for me, that you want to get to know me better.

Career & Skills

All my education and professional work is related to IT.
I graduated from The Higher School of Economics and Computer Science in Cracow, majoring in Network Administration. My first steps as an IT Specialist I made in "Zielona Sowa" Publishing House, where I worked as a Helpdesk specialist - first line of support. This work taught me how to deal with difficult situations and let me help others, which was very satisfying and inspiring. I had the opportunity to work with modern hardware and to face for the first time lines of HTML code as the administrator of the website.

In 2011 I took a job as a Branch Manager at A.PL INTERNET S.A. Here I began my adventure with Internet marketing. It is said that on the Internet we can sell everything. I agree with this opinion, so it should not come as a surprise to anyone that without hesitation I took up sales of FMCG products on the Internet. I like to take up new, often inspiring challenges. As a Branch Manager, I learned how to effectively manage people and solve even the most difficult problems. The management of new technologies was another step in my development in IT and despite the fact that my job was not coding but integration of systems and implementing them in a real world, this really significantly raised my competence.

Since 2012, I have been a Manager for e-commerce online store selling a variety of nutrition products and supplements for animals. I made this store from the ground. From all aspects of IT to logistics, marketing, selling, etc. I am proud of my achievements and I look forward to seeing how the store evolves and meets the needs of its customers.

In 2015, I completed my education and started series of training courses, preparing me to the programmer and software tester profession.

My daily work continuously provides me with new knowledge and challenges in the field of software and hardware testing. In my work, I deal with manual tests and prepare automatic tests (Selenium Webdriver, Rest-Assured using JAVA and PHP) and also use many useful tools for testers and programmers.

Software Testing Skills

Software testing has always reminded me of the most important and interesting part of the entire software development process, because of my innate curiosity and inquisitiveness. I liked to debug programs, applications and systems when I was a teenager. These skills are also useful to me in the present job. I am a perfectionist because I know that you can never find all the bugs in the software but that does not mean that you should not look for them.

I am a certified software tester and holder of the International Software Testing Qualifications Board Certification. I have ISTQB Foundation Level Certificate which I obtained in 2015. In order to increase my knowledge and professional competence in software testing, I attended the course with test automation in one of the most popular tool of this type - Selenium WebDriver. To test automation I use the Python object orientation language.

Since 2016 I have been working professionally as a QA Software Engineer and I try to develop my knowledge every day both at work and in leisure time. Software testing has become my passion and in this profession I would like to develope my career.
